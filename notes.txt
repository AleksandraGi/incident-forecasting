The baseline Logistic Regression model performed very well on the selected NAB time series. On the chronological test split, it achieved precision 1.00, recall 0.888, and F1-score 0.941 for the positive class. This means the model produced no false alarms on the test set and detected most upcoming incidents. A likely reason is that the machine temperature series contains a strong pre-incident pattern. At the same time, sliding-window samples overlap heavily, so neighboring examples are highly correlated, which may make the evaluation somewhat optimistic.
